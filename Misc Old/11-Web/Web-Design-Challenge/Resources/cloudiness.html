Summary: Latitude vs. Cloudiness

Per my research I determined that there is no correlation between a locations proximity to the equator and the level of clouds present. It is interesting to see that most of the plots form what appear to be lines around rounded number points. This could just be because cloudiness is more easily defined in increments than specifics. i.e. 40% cloudy vs 47% cloudy.
